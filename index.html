When Gambling Becomes a Problem

Gambling should be fun, but sometimes it turns bad. Watch for signs like betting money you need for bills, chasing losses to win back what you lost, lying to family about how much you play, feeling restless or angry when not gambling, or borrowing cash to keep going. If wins don't excite you anymore or losses wreck your mood for days, that's a red flag. It sneaks up, starts as entertainment then takes over time and thoughts. Recognize it early, step back.
